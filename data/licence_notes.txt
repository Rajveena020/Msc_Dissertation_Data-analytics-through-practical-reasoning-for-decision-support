DATASET 5: OpenStreetMap (Berkshire)
================================================
Source: OpenStreetMap Foundation
URL: https://www.openstreetmap.org/copyright
Licence: Open Data Commons Open Database Licence (ODbL) v1.0
Licence URL: https://opendatacommons.org/licenses/odbl/1-0/

PERMISSIONS:
- Copy, distribute and use the database
- Produce works from the database
- Modify, transform and build upon the database

OBLIGATIONS (CRITICAL - causes conflict with OGL):
- Any derived database must be published under ODbL
- Must keep the database open
- Must attribute OpenStreetMap contributors

PROHIBITIONS:
- Cannot make a derived database closed/proprietary
- Cannot combine with a licence that restricts redistribution

ODRL KEY: odbl_v1
